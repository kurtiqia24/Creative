Understanding positions

Soccer has numerouse positions. It ranges from 3 sections of the field, offense, midfield, and defense. The defense is primarily made up of 3-5 players, most commonly 4 including a left back, right back and 2 centerbacks and of course a goalie. Midfield can be made up of left/right mids, and center(attacking/defending) mid. Fianally the attacking 3rd can be comprised of left/right wings, strikers or center forwards. 11 players are allowed to fill these positions at once on the field and it's up to the coach to decide that
